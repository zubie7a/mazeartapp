App made by Santiago/zubie7a. Check Instagram @mazeartapp for more art!

Thanks to Javier/infrahumano and Santiago/moebio for the playful exchanges and inspiration during the lockdowns.

Keyboard controls

  • Z – New grid
  • R – Reapply current palette
  • T – Random palette
  • F – Toggle vertical mirroring
  • G – Toggle horizontal mirroring
  • H – Toggle smoothing
